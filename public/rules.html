Proctored Exam Instructions

Welcome to your proctored exam! Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the exam.

General Instructions

  • Ensure that you have a stable internet connection throughout the duration of the exam.
  • Use only the approved browser (e.g., Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox) for the exam.
  • Keep your webcam and microphone turned on at all times during the exam for monitoring purposes.
  • Do not open any other applications, websites, or devices during the exam.
  • No external materials (notes, books, or other aids) are allowed unless explicitly stated.

Identification & Authentication

  • You must present a valid ID to the proctor at the beginning of the exam.
  • Ensure your face is clearly visible on the webcam at all times.

Exam Environment

  • Take the exam in a quiet and well-lit room where there will be no disturbances.
  • Your desk must be clear of any unauthorized materials.
  • Make sure no other individuals are present in the room during the exam.

During the Exam

  • If you encounter technical difficulties, immediately notify the proctor through the provided communication channel.
  • Refrain from any form of cheating. Violations will result in disqualification and further disciplinary actions.
  • Do not leave your seat without prior permission from the proctor.

Exam Timing

  • The exam is timed. Make sure to manage your time effectively.
  • The exam will automatically submit when the time limit is reached.
Note: Any suspicious behavior or actions may result in immediate termination of the exam.
